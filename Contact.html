Company Info :-

Shree Marutinandan Restaurants today, is not only the most spread out chain restaurants of Ahmedabad but also has successfully carved out its identity as the most authentic Kathiyawadi cuisine serving restaurants. Apart from the same you will also find the wide range of Punjabi, Chinese and selected delicacies from Rajasthani and Continental cuisines.The reason behind choosing Kathiyawadi cuisine to start with was very simple. Amdavadis loved Kathiyawadi cuisine but there were many few serving the delicacies with authentic taste. The early few of the restaurants that were opened(still serving to guests) did not only serve the real taste but also had a traditional Khaatla-Bethak sitting arrangement that became an identity of the restaurants. Today, from the 16 branches many have a modern day aesthetic interiors and ambience, banquet hall and banquet gardens. Over the years each of our effort, from introducing the new delicacy or cuisine up to opening a new branch with a unique interiors have all been received by love with all the taste lovers. Today with restaurants at almost all the corners of the Ahmedabad we would like to say A Big Thank You for supporting and loving us.
